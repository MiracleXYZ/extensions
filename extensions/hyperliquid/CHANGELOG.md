# Hyperliquid Changelog

## [Initial Version] - 2026-07-22

- Browse perp markets with live prices, 24h change, funding and volume, plus a volume / open-interest overview header
- Market views: All, Favorites, Top Gainers, Top Losers and Highest Funding
- Candlestick chart (1h / 4h / 1d) and stats in market detail, with entry and liquidation price overlays when you hold the coin
- Track open positions across multiple labeled wallets with live PnL, ROE, leverage, entry, mark and liquidation prices
- Account margin-health readout and liquidation-risk flags on positions
- Portfolio Performance chart: equity / PnL over 1D / 1W / 1M / All Time
- Favorite markets and pin live tickers to the menu bar, with a menu-bar alert when a position nears liquidation
- "Default Chart Interval" and "Liquidation Alert Distance" preferences
